Our Story
20+ Years of Trusted HVAC Service

Farmington Hills Furnace LLC has an amazing reputation in the Farmington Hills MI area and has over 20 years of experience in HVAC. We are a full-service company that can help with all of your heating and cooling needs — furnace repair, AC repair, installation, humidifiers, and air filtration units.

We work with both residential and commercial heating and cooling in Farmington Hills MI, as well as the entire Metro Detroit area. We offer 24-hour emergency service and will get your heating and cooling problems fixed quickly and for a reasonable price.

Most of our clients are either repeat customers from over the years or are family and friends being referred to us by our current customers. After continuously getting referrals for all these years it tells us we must be doing something right. Our motto is to provide great HVAC service at a great price — we want to be your heating and cooling company for life.
