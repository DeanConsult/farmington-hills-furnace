Furnace Repair

When you have a broken furnace and it's cold outside, nothing can be worse. We have agents standing by 24/7 to take your calls for emergency service. Furnace repair is our specialty and our heating and cooling specialists are ready to come out and fix your broken furnace.

At Farmington Hills Furnace LLC, it is our goal to give you all the information you need to make an informed decision on the best strategy for investing in your furnace — not just sell you a new one. Call us and we'll come out within 24–48 hours.

Furnace Installation

Hearing the news that you need a new furnace can be painful for your pocketbook and many times comes on suddenly. At Farmington Hills Furnace LLC, we offer financing options so you can pay over time and not face a major upfront cost.

With new technology and efficiency in today's furnaces, you should also save money on your heating bills over time. We use only top brand name manufacturers offering the best warranties and reliability. Call us today for a free estimate.

Air Conditioner Repair

In the summer when it's hot and you have a broken AC, we are just a phone call away. Many times, a broken AC is an easy fix like just adding some freon to your unit. Our technicians typically keep most parts on their trucks to fix a broken AC quickly.

We are specialists at AC repair and all of our contractors are experts at all types of air conditioning units. If you have a broken AC, contact us as soon as possible — letting the system run with a mechanical problem can sometimes cause more damage.

Air Conditioner Installation

When your air conditioner has finally given up and it's the middle of summer, your friends at Farmington Hills Furnace and Air Conditioner are here for you. Our specialists will discuss several options based on your budget and cooling needs.

Our goal is to get your home to a comfortable temperature as quickly as possible and at a price you can afford. Please give us a call today for a free consultation.

Air Conditioner Maintenance

Maintaining your air conditioner is an extremely important part of home ownership. In the hot summer months in Farmington Hills, your AC unit is working overtime to keep your home comfortable. All of this strain affects a variety of components within the unit.

Things like filters, coils, coil fins, condensate drains, and freon should be monitored and updated on a consistent basis. Our AC service technicians are experts in all of these repairs and more — call us and we'll let you know exactly what needs attention, and what doesn't.

24/7 Emergency Service

We understand that your heating and cooling needs don't just occur between 9am and 5pm Monday–Friday. If you are in need of furnace repair or air conditioning repair, we are available to you on a 24-hour basis.

There are many individuals who for medical reasons cannot tolerate excessive heat or cold even for a short time. At Farmington Hills Furnace LLC, we are here for you and will get a professional technician out to your home or business quickly.
